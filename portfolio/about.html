Jialu Yu, currently working as a UI/UX designer/developer and project manager at Netfonds AG.

After finishing her studies, Jialu worked in a finance consulting company, Netfonds AG for already four years till now. The first year worked she just as UI/UX designer and then also as project manager and the last two years also as frontend developer. She is enthusiastic in design and creativity and works several years as a freelancer besides her studies and job.
